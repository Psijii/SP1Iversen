OUR EVENTS

Night at the museum

During November and December 2022 we host Night at the museum!

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

We host Night at the museum every weekend, from Saturday to Sunday until the 17th of December.

Energetica exhibition

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to SCI mu. It's a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them.

This event is available until March 2023.
