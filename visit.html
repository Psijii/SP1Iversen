Our location

How to get here, and where to eat? We have made it easier for you and gathered all the practical information you need on this page.

The SCI mu is located at

Muséplassen 1, 5007 Bergen

Admission

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 50 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

We have step-free entrance, and elevators to every floor

If you encounter any problems during your visit, please do not hestitate to contact us for your feedback.

Café

Our caféteria at the museum is well-stocked with delicious dishes suitable for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Planning an event? You can book our caféteria for weddings, birthdays and any other event you can think of! For weddings we will make arrangements for you and your significant other to take weddings photos in our museum.

Please contact us for any questions.

Book a tour

With guided tours tickets you and your group receive a free handy booklet that explains the curiosity cabinets at our museum. The booklet has thorough explanation of all our exhibitions, ranging from Mesolithic archaeology to science and physics in space.

Payment for the tour will be at the museum.
